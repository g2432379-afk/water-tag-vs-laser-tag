Water Tag vs Laser Tag: Equipment Comparison for Camps & Events

Short answer: if your program runs outdoors in warm weather and the equipment needs to
move between sites, mobile Water Tag is the closer fit. If you operate a fixed indoor
arena, laser tag is built for that. site: https://watertag.com/

This guide compares the two for camp directors, resort operators and event organizers.

When to choose Water Tag

You run a summer camp, resort, school or mobile event business
You want one kit that travels between locations across a season
Your programming is outdoor and warm-weather, where water is part of the appeal

When to choose laser tag

You operate a fixed indoor arena or party venue
Play happens in a controlled, climate-stable space year-round
